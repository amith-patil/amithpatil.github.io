Back in 2010, I got my first computer and was immediately mesmerized by the technology. This evolved into me exploring the realm of Computers where I taught myself to build, troubleshoot and code.

I graduated with a Bachelors degree in Computer Engineering in 2016, and worked as a Support Engineer at Dell EMC, where I explored working with NAS servers, Linux distributed Systems and Web development.

3 Years ago, I joined Google as one of their first Solutions Enginner for a new CyberSecurity Offering known as Google Secops ( formerly Google Chronicle ) in the APAC/EMEA Region.

Outside of work, I enjoy Video games, Motorcycles,Videography and hanging out with my Wife and Cat.
